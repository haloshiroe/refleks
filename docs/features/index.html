Features

Everything you need

Understand and improve your aim training - automatically.

Automatic Tracking

RefleK's monitors your KovaaK's stats folder and imports new runs automatically. No clicking, no uploading - just play and your data appears. Score, accuracy, TTK, kills per minute, and more are all tracked.

Practice Sessions

Runs are grouped automatically based on timing. Review whole training blocks, see aggregated performance, get fatigue alerts when it's time for a break, and track your daily activity patterns.

Scenario Analysis

TTK moving averages, accuracy vs speed charts, and events over time visualizations for every run.

100% Private

Everything runs locally. No accounts, no tracking, no cloud uploads. Your data stays on your computer.

Built-in Themes

Dark, Light, Nord, Dracula, Gruvbox, Ocean, Solarized, Midnight, and more.

Benchmark Progress

Browse official KovaaK's benchmarks and track your progress with live updates. Get smart recommendations on which scenarios to focus next. Launch playlists directly from the app.

Mouse Movement Analysis

Record and visualize your mouse movements during scenarios. Automatic detection of overshoots, undershoots, and inefficient mouse paths. Get sensitivity suggestions based on your aiming patterns.

Share Progress

One-click share copies a polished benchmark progress image to your clipboard.

Fatigue Detection

Get alerts when your performance is declining - know when to take a break.

Auto Updates

Check for updates and install them with one click, right from the app.

Free & Open Source

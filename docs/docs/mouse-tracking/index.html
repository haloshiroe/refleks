Mouse Movement Recording

Record and analyze your mouse movements during scenarios.

Overview

RefleK's can record your mouse movements during KovaaK's scenarios. Replay your runs, analyze your mouse paths, and get insights into your aiming patterns.

Enable Mouse Tracking

Mouse tracking is disabled by default. To enable it:

  1. Open Settings (gear icon)
  2. Go to General → Mouse Tracking
  3. Toggle it on

How It Works

When mouse tracking is enabled:

  • RefleK's maintains a rolling buffer of mouse movements while KovaaK's is running
  • Mouse position is captured at high frequency using Windows Raw Input
  • When a scenario ends, the relevant portion is extracted from the buffer and saved with your run
  • Data is stored locally with your run stats
Anticheat Warning: Mouse tracking uses low-level Windows input APIs (Raw Input). While this is safe for KovaaK's, some kernel-level anticheats in other games may flag this type of input monitoring. If you play games with aggressive anticheats (like Vanguard or FACEIT), consider disabling mouse tracking or closing RefleK's before launching those games.

Playback & Analysis

After a run, you can view the recording:

  • Replay: Watch your mouse path with play/pause controls
  • Speed control: Slow down or speed up playback
  • Scrubbing: Jump to any point in the recording
  • Path visualization: See your entire mouse path

Automatic Insights

RefleK's automatically analyzes your mouse movements:

  • Overshoots: When you move past the target
  • Undershoots: When you don't reach the target
  • Inefficient paths: Curved or wobbly movements

Sensitivity Training Suggestions

Based on your overshoot and undershoot patterns, RefleK's suggests a training sensitivity to help you improve:

  • Frequent overshoots: Suggests a higher sensitivity to train with smaller, more controlled movements
  • Frequent undershoots: Suggests a lower sensitivity to train with larger, more confident flicks

This is counterintuitive by design - the goal is to train your muscle memory to compensate for your current weaknesses. After 5-10 runs at the suggested sensitivity, switch back to your normal sens. You'll likely find your movements more refined.

Important: This is a training sensitivity, not a permanent setting. The idea is to temporarily exaggerate the opposite of your problem to train better motor control.
Privacy: All recordings are stored locally on your computer. Nothing is ever uploaded.

Performance

Mouse tracking is optimized to have minimal impact on your system:

  • Recording doesn't affect your in-game sensitivity
  • Minimal CPU and memory usage
  • No impact on game performance
Note: Mouse tracking is Windows-only due to platform-specific APIs.

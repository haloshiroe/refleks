RefleK's

Your aim trainer gives scores. RefleK's gives answers.

RefleK's is a free, open‑source desktop app that automatically tracks your KovaaK's practice sessions, shows what you're improving at, and helps you see where to focus next — all running on your computer with zero setup.

Common questions

Is RefleK's made by KovaaK's?
No. RefleK's is an independent project made by the community. We're not affiliated with KovaaK's or The Meta.
Is RefleK's safe?
Yes. RefleK's is open‑source and runs entirely on your computer. It doesn't collect data or send your stats anywhere (except when you choose to fetch benchmark progress from KovaaK's).
What platforms work with RefleK's?
RefleK's currently supports Windows. The mouse tracking feature is Windows‑only because it uses Windows‑specific APIs. If you'd like support for Mac or Linux, let us know on GitHub.
Does it track my mouse all the time?
No. Mouse tracking is turned off by default. When you turn it on, it only records during KovaaK's scenarios and saves everything to your computer. You can turn it off anytime in settings.
How do I help improve RefleK's?
Open an issue on GitHub to report bugs or suggest features. If you want to contribute code, check CONTRIBUTING.md in the repository for guidelines.
Where do I report bugs?
Open an issue on GitHub with details about what happened. You can also message arm8 on Discord if you need help.
How does benchmark tracking work?
RefleK's includes all the major benchmark lists. When you view a benchmark, it fetches your progress from KovaaK's using your Steam account (detected automatically on Windows).
Do I need to create an account?
No. RefleK's doesn't have accounts or logins. Everything runs on your computer and uses your existing Steam account for benchmark progress.
